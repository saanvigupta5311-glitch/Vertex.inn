Our Story

I am Saanvi Gupta, a student of Class 10 studying at Sacred Heart Convent School, Sarabha Nagar, Ludhiana.

I built Vertex with one motto— every student should get the opportunity to work on real-life projects, and businesses should find reliable and affordable freelancers.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between education and industry, allowing students to develop practical skills while helping businesses grow through talented young minds.
